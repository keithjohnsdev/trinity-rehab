OUR SERVICES

PHYSICAL
THERAPY

Physical Therapists work to improve gait, balance, transfer, bed mobility and other gross motor skills.

OCCUPATIONAL
THERAPY

Occupational Therapy focuses on fine motor skills, sensory motor skills, and visual motor skills that promote independence in activities of daily living and improve functional abilities and socialization at home, school, and their community.

SPEECH
THERAPY

Speech-Language therapists assist people with communication disorders (speech and language impairments), cognitive-communication disorders, voice disorders, and swallowing disorders.
